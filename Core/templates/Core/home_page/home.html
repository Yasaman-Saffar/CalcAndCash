How It Works

Join the contest in a few simple steps.

Create a Team

Players can create a team. The creator automatically becomes the team leader.

Invite Members

Leaders can invite users. Invited users can accept requests from their messages.

Solve & Trade

Buy questions, solve them for rewards, or trade questions with other teams.

Highest Asset Wins

The team with the highest Total Asset at the end becomes the winner.

Gameplay

Points, items, inflation, and bank interest

Teams use their initial points to buy questions and items. Correct answers give more points than the cost of the question.

Teams can also buy and sell items with the bank. Items prices may change because of positive or negative inflation. Inflation times are hidden, but bank interest is applied at known intervals.

Contest Rules

A quick overview of the most important rules.

The team creator becomes the leader. The leader can invite members, and accepted members can access the team dashboard. Leadership can be transferred to another member.

Each team starts with 1000 points and can spend them on questions and items. Solving questions correctly increases the team's points.

Teams may trade unsolved questions with other teams. The price is agreed by both sides. A one-time code is sent only to team leaders through messages to confirm the trade.

The final score is called Total Asset. It is calculated from the team's bank balance and the current value of its items. The team with the highest Total Asset wins. If needed, access to contest pages may be temporarily restricted.
